ToddyTyper

ToddyTyper is a way for your toddler to play with the keyboard, while reducing the chances of changing settings, deleting programs or erasing your hard drive.

When you click the button below, ToddyTyper will launch at full screen. Little fingers won't easily exit out of the pogram this way. Just click the 'X' in the top right corner to exit, or press the 'ESC' button.

ToddyTyper is not made for mobile devices.
